\begin{frame}{Conclusion}

In non-blocking algorithms, the \texttt{Help(d, n)} routine is strictly necessary only to guarantee liveness. Disabling it has no effect on the specification without impacting any safety invariants.

Lightweight reading protocol implies linearizability with minimal overhead.
